About CounselMore

At CounselMore, we are passionate about empowering college counselors to help students navigate the college application process more effectively. Our platform was built to streamline every aspect of the counseling process, from managing student profiles to tracking application deadlines and everything in between.

We understand that counseling can be overwhelming, especially when managing large numbers of students with diverse needs. That's why we've designed CounselMore to save time, reduce stress, and ensure that counselors can focus on what truly matters—guiding their students toward success.

Our tools are designed to be intuitive and easy to use, allowing counselors to manage student information, track applications, and build college lists with ease. With CounselMore, you're equipped to provide a higher level of service to every student you work with.
